01 · Executive Summary

Over 90 days, Tiny Hub routed real kilowatt-hours between residential generators and consumers across 12 McHenry County communities, settling every five-minute batch on Arbitrum L2. The pilot demonstrated that peer routing on existing ComEd feeders is technically feasible, economically meaningful to participants, and operationally compatible with utility reliability obligations.
