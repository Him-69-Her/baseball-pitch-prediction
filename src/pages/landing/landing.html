How it works

From rooftop to neighbor in three steps.

The engine reads the grid, matches by physics, and settles by contract. No middlemen, no guesswork.

01 / Meter
Stream telemetry

Smart meters and inverters publish kWh and quality of supply at 15-second cadence to the matching engine.

02 / Match
Solve for the grid

A deterministic solver pairs producers with neighbors using line losses, voltage and topology — not just price.

03 / Settle
On-chain receipts

Trades clear to the L2 marketplace contract. Every kWh is auditable, programmable, and yours.

Thesis

The grid is a graph.
Markets should respect it.

Most energy markets ignore the wires. Tiny Hub is built on the premise that the physical grid — its topology, losses, and constraints — should shape every trade.

The result is a marketplace where local production stays local, congestion is priced honestly, and every settlement is reproducible from first principles.
